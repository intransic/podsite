About Waveform

The Show

Waveform exists because audio storytelling is having a moment — and that moment is worth examining closely. We are a podcast for people who love podcasts, made by people who think carefully about what makes the medium work.

Our Voice

We are curious and opinionated, but not prescriptive. We believe great audio can take many forms, and we are interested in the specific choices that make each form land. Expect honest conversations, occasional disagreements, and a bias toward the craft details that usually go unspoken.

The Host

Waveform is hosted by Jordan Reeve, a producer and audio journalist with over a decade of experience across public radio, narrative documentary, and independent podcasting. Jordan started the show to have the conversations about audio craft that were not happening anywhere else.
